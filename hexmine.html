Koorma's Flowers

Hello and welcome, new apprentice to Koorma the turtle! Let me be first to say congratulations on the new position, there were many qualified candidates and you stood out from the pack. Now, as you stated in your cover letter, you've always wanted to travel the world and have also enjoyed gardening for some time now. That's precisely what you'll be doing here as an apprentice gardener on Koorma's shell!


As you can see below, before you is an untamed wilderness of dark green unknown vegetation. Among this vegetation, though, are a number of Middlemist Red flowers. These, rarest of flowers, only grow in China and on Koorma's back. These flowers are both delicate and aromatic. From the space you start on, you will be able to smell how many are around you, but be cautious! If you touch these flowers without the right-click of your mouse, they will wither, as they are not big fans of the left-click. Gather (by either right-clicking or leaving alone) as many of these flowers as you can for glory, profit, and sustinance for Koorma!


Since you're new here, we'll start with Koorma swimming low, so there are only a few Middlemist Reds present, but each time you find them all, Koorma will swim a little higher and expose more area for you to explore!

  New Season  

A quick tutorial: to start the game, left-click on any space (don't worry you won't hurt any flowers on your first guess). From here, each space will show the number of flowers adjacent to it. To remind yourself where flowers are, you can right-click (or ctrl+click for our Mac-user friends) on their location so that you will not left-click on them in future moves. To finish the game, uncover all of the spaces except for the flowers! You may move your view relative to Koorma by left-clicking and dragging. Each new season, new flowers and other vegeation grow and Koorma swims a bit further out of the water. Collect as many flowers as you can!
